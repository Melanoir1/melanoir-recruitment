## 1) 학교 커뮤니티용 짧은 버전

### [대학생 모집] 멜라누아 AI 마케팅 챌린저 1기

AI로 콘텐츠 만들고, 실제 브랜드 SNS에 업로드해볼 대학생 팀원을 모집합니다.

- 활동명: `AI 마케팅 챌린저`
- 활동 내용: AI 모델 활용 SNS 콘텐츠 기획/제작/업로드
- 채널: Instagram, Threads, X, LinkedIn
- 활동 기간: `8주` (예시, 운영 상황에 따라 조정 가능)
- 모집 대상: 대학(재)학생/휴학생

#### 이런 분 환영합니다
- 마케팅/브랜딩/SNS에 관심 있는 분
- AI 툴로 결과물을 빠르게 만들어보고 싶은 분
- 팀으로 실행하고 피드백 받는 걸 좋아하는 분

#### 혜택
- 실무형 포트폴리오
- 멜라누아 팀 피드백
- 수료증/우수자 리워드
- 우수자 인턴 연계 검토

#### 지원 방법
- 모집 기간: `YYYY.MM.DD ~ YYYY.MM.DD`
- 지원 링크: `URL 입력`
- 문의: `이메일/오픈채팅 입력`

한 줄 소개:  
**AI를 잘 쓰는 사람보다, AI로 실제 결과를 내는 사람을 찾습니다.**

## 2) LinkedIn 공고 버전

## Melanoir | AI Marketing Challenger Program (Campus)

Melanoir is launching the **AI Marketing Challenger** program for university students who want to build real marketing outcomes with AI.

This is not a theory-based program. Participants will plan, produce, and publish content across Melanoir social channels using AI tools, then iterate based on performance data.

### What you will do
- Plan social content for Melanoir (`Instagram`, `Threads`, `X`, `LinkedIn`)
- Use AI models/tools to generate and improve copy and visuals
- Adapt content by platform format and audience behavior
- Publish selected content and monitor engagement metrics
- Present weekly insights and optimization proposals

### Who we are looking for
- University students (enrolled or on leave)
- Strong interest in marketing, branding, and social content
- Comfortable experimenting with AI tools in a practical workflow
- Self-driven, collaborative, and execution-oriented

### Preferred
- Experience managing social channels or creator accounts
- Portfolio in short-form video, card news, or campaign content
- Familiarity with tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Canva, CapCut, Notion
- Basic ability to summarize data and extract insights

### Program format
- Duration: `8 weeks` (adjustable)
- Mode: Online-based, with optional offline sessions
- Team structure: Small project squads
- Outcome: Real published content + final review presentation

### Benefits
- Hands-on mentorship from Melanoir team
- Portfolio with real brand execution
- Certificate of completion / rewards for outstanding performance
- Potential follow-up opportunities (internship/project collaboration)

### Application process
1. Application form
2. Short assignment (AI-based content sample)
3. Online interview
4. Final selection

Apply: `URL 입력`  
Application period: `YYYY.MM.DD ~ YYYY.MM.DD`  
Contact: `EMAIL 입력`
